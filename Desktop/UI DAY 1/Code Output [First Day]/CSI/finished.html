About

The Department of Computer Science - Center for Students Innovations is a service-oriented organization aimed towards the enhancement of students learning by working with real-world projects.

Mission

  1. Create development camps wherein partners can sponsor students in enriching their skills in Software Engineering.
  2. Get a diverse set of software projects for CSI to develop.
  3. Showcase the quality projects DCS and CSI produces and make them reachable by the public.
